About RecruitSmart

RecruitSmart is a modern job portal built using the MERN stack that simplifies hiring through automation and AI-driven interviews.

It bridges the gap between candidates and recruiters by offering a smooth, transparent, and intelligent hiring experience.

Manage candidates, resumes, and decisions easily.
